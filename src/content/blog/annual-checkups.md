Prevention is the first step toward a healthy life. Annual medical check-ups help detect potential problems before they become serious.

These check-ups include basic exams, clinical tests, and specific evaluations based on the patient’s age and medical history.

Performing them regularly reduces risks and allows for comprehensive long-term health monitoring.
