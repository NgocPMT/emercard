Emotional well-being directly affects physical health. Stress, anxiety, or exhaustion can impact work performance and daily life.

That is why mental health programs, including psychological care, workshops, and therapeutic support, are essential in any modern health plan.

Talking about mental health means talking about holistic well-being.
